About Me

Hello! I'm Mbogo Eston Kimani, a self-taught UI/UX designer and certified full-stack developer specializing in front-end development. I have a keen eye for web design and strive to create visually stunning and user-friendly interfaces.

I've honed my skills through a variety of online courses from reputable institutions such as FreeCodeCamp, Udemy, and Google Certifications. With certificates in hand, I'm equipped with the knowledge and expertise to bring innovative ideas to life.

I've actively participated in various online and physical communities, such as the GitHub and Figma communities, where I've collaborated with other professionals and enthusiasts, shared insights, and contributed to open-source projects.

In the future, I aspire to continue pushing the boundaries of web design and development, further refining my skills and expertise. My goal is to become a leading figure in the UI/UX design and front-end development industry, creating impactful and memorable digital experiences for users worldwide.

Design Tools

  • Figma is a collaborative interface design tool used for creating interactive prototypes, wireframes, and user interface designs for websites and applications. Its cloud-based platform enables real-time collaboration among team members, making it ideal for designing and iterating on complex projects.
  • Adobe Illustrator is a versatile graphic design software widely used for creating and editing images, illustrations, and graphics. It offers advanced features for photo manipulation, digital painting, and graphic design, making it suitable for designing logos, editing photos, and creating mockups for websites and applications.
  • Canva is a user-friendly online graphic design platform that offers a wide range of tools and templates for creating professional-looking graphics, including logos, social media posts, presentations, and videos.
